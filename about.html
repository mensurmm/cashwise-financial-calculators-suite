About Cash Wise

Our Mission

Cash-Wise is designed to simplify everyday financial tasks. From VAT and tax calculators to savings and deposit tools, we help you make quick, accurate decisions while learning the basics of money management, specially our goal is to empower users in Ethiopia with accessible financial tools using AI.

Our Story

Founded in Addis Ababa University, Cash-Wise was created to empower students, professionals, and everyday citizens with easy-to-use financial tools. We believe financial and Tax literacy should be simple, accessible, and empowering.

Cash-Wise

Your Smart Financial Toolkit — CashWise is an interactive web application designed to simplify everyday financial and mathematical tasks. It provides a suite of easy-to-use calculators, including VAT, income tax, simple interest, compound interest, fixed deposit (FD), recurring deposit (RD), and a scientific calculator. With a clean design, responsive layout, and real-time results powered by HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, CashWise helps users make quick, accurate financial decisions while learning the basics of money management.
